UI Designer - Non-Remote

Full Time - Marketing - Sahiwal, PK

About Us

We are Services Ground, a digital marketing and design agency helping brands that do good, look good - put simply, we work with businesses making a difference through branding, digital experiences, and social. Our clients include sustainable fashion and sportswear brands, international seaweed farms, billion dollar AI companies, and charitable initiatives. We are a young team, with big ambitions to make a meaningful difference through the work we love.

Our Team

You'll be working alongside digital designers, videographers, developers, account managers and strategists. Our office near Notting Hill at Great Western Studios, with windows out onto the canal. We work in an informal, very collaborative, fast-paced environment. We all get along very well, no two days are the same - and we all have high standards for the work we deliver because we all love what we do.

The Role

You just get it now - you’ve tamed Figma, built brand-led design systems, and know how to get to the heart of your user’s problems... what’s the next big challenge? Vovi is looking for a UI designer to join the creation of award-winning online user experiences. The ideal designer will feel comfortable leading clients from wireframe to prototype, producing detailed UI component libraries and defining intricate interactions. You’ll be working alongside our Head of Website, Creative Director and collaborating with in-house developers and other designers to ideate, create and iterate - this is a mid weight role for an ambitious candidate, so if you’re ready to take things to the next level, you’ve come to right place.

Requirements

What you'll do
  • Lead the design of cutting edge websites, platforms and apps.
  • Determine design requirements based on their briefs
  • Pitch ideas concisely to the team and to clients.
  • Create responsive wireframes, high fidelity designs, and prototypes in Figma
  • Help define eye catching UI interactions with After Effects or Principle
  • Co-lead user experience workshops with clients, defining their problems and mapping user journeys
  • Join internal & external brainstorming and collaborative workshops
  • Occasionally take an active involvement in other branding, social media, and video projects from around the studio.
What you need:
  • A sense of humour
  • 2+ years of experience designing websites
  • Wicked portfolio of websites and UX/UI projects you've personally designed
  • Expert knowledge of Figma and design system creation
  • Confidence generating ideas & concepts that combine a creative and strategic understanding
  • You are great at organising and prioritising tasks
  • Located in London to work in-house with the Vovi team
Preferred/Bonus Skills
  • Strong understanding/experience with web animations
  • PLUS - experience animating your UI in Figma, Principle or AE (or equivalent)
  • Submission of works to Behance, Dribbble, Awwwards & CSSDA
Who you are
  • You bring the fire each and every day - to brainstorms, workshops, lunchtime chat. We do this for the love of it, and want to be surrounded by like minded people!
  • You want to win design awards... for every project.
  • You like working in a fast-paced environment and keep your finger on the pulse, keeping your designs current and cutting edge
  • You believe growth comes from frequent thoughtful, constructive criticism

Remuneration

£32,000 - £38,000 depending on experience and quality of portfolio

Benefits

  • Fantastic options scheme available
  • Unlimited holiday
  • Discounted gym membership and health plan,
  • Sustainability focused pension with NEST
  • Employee incentivisation scheme.
